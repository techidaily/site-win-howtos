## Fix 1: Roll Back Drivers

 If the error occurs after you install new drivers, you can try restoring the drivers to the previous version.

 1) Go to[Device Manager](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) and expand the category to locate the device with problematic drivers.
