![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/img_599a434085d58.png)

Thus if your Skype video stop working, like your video is black or freezing, it would be troublesome. However, there’s no problem can’t be figured out. Don’t worry if you are facing the trouble.  1)  

 Log in Skype with your account.  
 Click**Tools** on menu bar in Skype window.  
 Then choose**Options…**
