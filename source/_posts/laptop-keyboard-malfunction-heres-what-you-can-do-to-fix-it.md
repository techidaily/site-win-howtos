## How do I fix “laptop keyboard not working”?

Following are 3 quick fixes you can try to fix your problem. Work from the top of the list until you find the one that works.

1. [Restart your laptop](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/)
2. [Remove the battery](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/)
3. [Update your keyboard driver](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/)

## Fix 1: Restart your laptop

You can solve many computer woes by a simple restart. If your laptop keyboard can’t works, reboot your laptop as the first quick fix. To restart your laptop, you can use the**restart option from the Start menu**via a USB mouse connecting with your laptop. Or use the power button on your laptop to shutdown first and boot it again.
