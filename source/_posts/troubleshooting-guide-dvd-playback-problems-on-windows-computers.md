 One of the first things you should check when something is off with your keyboard is the connection between it and your computer.

* If you‚Äôre using a USB keyboard, you should connect your keyboard directly to your PC without a USB hub.
* If you‚Äôre on a desktop, try the USB port at the back of your PC mainframe.
* If you‚Äôre using a wireless keyboard, make sure that the batteries in your keyboard are working, or try to use new batteries.
