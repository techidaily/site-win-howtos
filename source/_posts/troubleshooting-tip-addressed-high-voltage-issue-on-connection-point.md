### Fix 1: Disconnect ALL USB devices and re-seat your battery

 Sometimes this**USB ports not working** problem is only a hiccup that can be easily solved by removing all the peripherals and reseating the battery before getting your HP laptop back to where it is.

Here‚Äôs how:

1. Remove**ALL** the USB devices from your HP laptop.
2. Power off your laptop and disconnect it from power supplies.
3. Carefully remove the battery from the back of the laptop. (You might also need to check the manual if you‚Äôre unsure how this is done).
4. Wait 10 minutes before you seat the battery back in.
