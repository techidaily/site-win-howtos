## Harmonizing Display and Timing - Monitor Support Now Restored

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/Snap58-1.jpg)

 Does your monitor screen go black and pop up with an error saying: _**The current input timing is not supported by the monitor display**_  ?

 This error occurs to your computer because your monitor fails to synchronize the input signals from your computer, or there is something wrong with your monitor connections. But don’t worry. We’ll help you fix the error and get your monitor back on track.

## Try these fixes

 Here are the solutions that have helped people resolve the same problem. Check it out:

1. [Change your monitor settings](#F1)
2. [Update graphics card driver](#F2)
3. [Boot your computer in low-resolution mode](#F3)

 Note: If you can’t log into your system GUI, you should first boot your computer into **[Safe Mode](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/)**  , then perform these instructions.
